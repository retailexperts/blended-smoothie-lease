TENANT PROTECTIONS & EXCLUSIVE RIGHTS

üõ°Ô∏è Your Exclusive Use Protection

You are the ONLY dedicated smoothie/juice bar in Chastain Hill retail center.

No Other Small Tenant (Under 5,000 SF) Can:

  • Operate substantially similar to a smoothie/juice bar
  • Derive more than 10% of sales from made-on-site smoothies
  • Derive more than 10% of sales from fresh-pressed juices
  • Derive more than 10% of sales from blended fruit bowls

Exceptions to Exclusive:

  • Existing tenants already in place (grandfathered)
  • Large format tenants over 5,000 SF (e.g., grocery stores)
  • Replacement tenants in same space as prior existing tenant

‚ö† Maintaining Your Exclusive

Protection remains as long as you're open and not in default. Lost if you stop operating for 60+ days or violate permitted use.
